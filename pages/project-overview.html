Project Overview

Understanding the Business Problem and Proposed Solution

The Business Problem

High Impact

Revenue Loss

30% commission paid to third-party delivery platforms

Average restaurant: $15,000/month lost

No Customer Data

Zero direct customer profiles

Platforms own all customer data and relationships

Inefficient Process

15% order errors due to manual handling

Phone orders cause miscommunication

Poor Customer Experience

20+ minute wait times during peak hours

No order tracking or status updates

The Foodora Solution

Proposed

System architecture details: System Architecture Overview (DOCX)

Mobile App for Customers

  • Browse menu with real-time availability
  • Customize orders with special instructions
  • Real-time order tracking and notifications
  • Loyalty program integration

Web Dashboard for Restaurant

  • Order management system
  • Kitchen display system integration
  • Customer database and analytics
  • Inventory management alerts

Project Scope & Boundaries

IN SCOPE

  • Customer mobile ordering application
  • Restaurant admin web dashboard
  • Basic payment integration (cash on pickup)
  • Order management and tracking
  • Customer profile management
  • Basic reporting and analytics

OUT OF SCOPE

  • Delivery logistics and driver management
  • Advanced payment gateways (credit card processing)
  • Multi-restaurant franchise management
  • Advanced predictive analytics
  • Integration with POS hardware
  • Multi-language support (Phase 2)
