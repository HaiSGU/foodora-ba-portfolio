Learnings

What this case study demonstrates as a BA

Highlights

  • Translating business controls (credit limit, approvals, pricing overrides) into testable rules.
  • Role-based access and governance in ERP-style workflows.
  • Traceability from requirements → rules → UAT.

Risks & trade-offs

  • Over-control reduces speed at order entry; under-control increases financial risk.
  • Too many pricing exceptions cause audit complexity.
  • Approval thresholds must be configurable by policy.
