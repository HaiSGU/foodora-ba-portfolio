SAP ERP (Mock) — BA Case Study

Simulated BA engagement: Order-to-Cash (O2C) with approvals, pricing, and auditability (example reference: SAP S/4HANA)

Objective

Build a recruiter-friendly BA case study for a mock SAP ERP Order-to-Cash (O2C) scope and produce a complete, interview-ready deliverables pack (requirements, rules, RTM, and UAT). To make the mock concrete, terminology aligns to an example reference platform: SAP S/4HANA (SD + FI-AR + FSCM Credit Management). This is still ERP-mock and vendor-proprietary content is not used.

Scope (Scenario)

Business

Order-to-Cash (O2C)

Sales order → delivery → billing → payment + credit/approval controls.

Focus: pricing, credit limit, cancellations/returns, audit trail.

Controls

Governance & RBAC

Roles, permissions, approvals, and traceability.

Designed to look like a real BA deliverables pack.

Key Deliverables

  • PRD / BRD / SRS (core documents)
  • Stakeholder Engagement + RACI
  • Requirements (FR/NFR) + Scope
  • Business Rules + Decision Tables
  • Roles & Permissions Matrix
  • Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM)
  • UAT Test Cases + UAT Test Summary Report & Sign-off (IEEE 829 aligned)
