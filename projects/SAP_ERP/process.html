BA Process

How the case study was structured (BABOK / real-world delivery style)

1) Discovery

  • Define business goals, stakeholders, and decision rights (RACI).
  • Confirm O2C scope boundaries and what is out-of-scope.
  • Identify controls: credit limit, pricing overrides, approvals, audit logging.

2) Requirements

  • Write functional requirements (FR) per O2C step.
  • Define NFRs for auditability, security, performance, and availability.
  • Document business rules + decision tables for pricing and credit controls.

3) Governance & Testing

  • Create a role/permission matrix (RBAC).
  • Build a traceability matrix (FR ↔ Rules ↔ UAT).
  • Write a compact UAT mini pack with end-to-end scenarios.
