Business Analysis Process

A detailed walkthrough of the BA approach for the mock SAP ERP O2C scope

Project Timeline & Phases

01

Discovery & Analysis

Focus: scope boundaries, stakeholders, and control points

  • Clarify O2C scope: order → delivery → billing → payment
  • Define decision rights (who can override price / release credit blocks)
  • Identify audit events needed for evidence and compliance
02

Requirements Definition

Focus: FR/NFR + business rules that are testable

  • Write functional requirements per O2C step and exception
  • Define NFRs for auditability, security, availability
  • Document pricing/credit decisions as decision tables
03

Solution Design & Traceability

Focus: align rules, roles, and acceptance evidence

  • Define RBAC and approval workflows (RACI-aligned)
  • Create RTM linking requirements ↔ rules ↔ UAT
  • Prepare artifacts for stakeholder review and sign-off
04

Validation & Handover

Focus: UAT readiness and approval

  • UAT test cases in an Excel template
  • UAT test summary + sign-off (IEEE 829 style)
  • Close gaps found during reviews (rules, permissions, edge cases)

Requirements Elicitation Techniques

Interviews

Sales/Finance/Operations interviews to surface approvals and exception paths.

Workshops

Align on business rules, thresholds, and decision tables (pricing/credit).

Scenario Walkthroughs

Validate end-to-end O2C scenarios including returns/cancellations and re-billing.

Document Review

Cross-check PRD/BRD/SRS alignment with RBAC, RTM, and UAT.

Process Modeling & Analysis

AS-IS (Common ERP Risk Areas)

Typical failure points:

  • Pricing overrides without documented approval thresholds
  • Credit checks handled inconsistently across teams
  • Returns/cancellations handled differently by user
  • Audit evidence incomplete or hard to retrieve

TO-BE (Defined O2C with Controls)

Process model: BPMN L0 (PNG)

Improvements:

  • Explicit approval gates and decision tables
  • RBAC boundaries for who can do what
  • Traceability to UAT (RTM + test pack)
  • Evidence-ready sign-off workflow
