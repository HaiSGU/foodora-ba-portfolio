Learnings

How I upgraded a diagram-only assignment into a BA case study

Key Takeaways

  • Consistency beats volume: the same actors/permissions should appear across UC, sequence, and activity diagrams.
  • Operational systems need auditability: inventory/cash features require reason codes, approvals, and logs.
  • Edge cases are where BA value shows: negative stock rules, cancelled transfers, partial returns, promo conflicts.
  • Recruiters want impact: even without real metrics, you can present measurable targets and validation plans.
